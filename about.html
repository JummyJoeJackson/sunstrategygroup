About Us

01 — Where We StartedOur Story

Sun Strategy Group was founded by a group of University of Toronto students in 2025 who saw a lack of opportunities for undergraduate students to gain real-world consulting experience. We believe that all businesses deserve access to high-quality strategic guidance, regardless of their size or budget. Through dedicated hard work and persistence we promise to deliver high-quality results to each client.

02 — How We WorkOur Approach

We operate on a project-based model, where each engagement is led by a dedicated team of students who bring fresh perspectives and innovative solutions to the table. Our approach is rooted in a deep understanding of business fundamentals, combined with a commitment to delivering measurable results. From market research to strategic planning, we work closely with our clients to develop tailored solutions that address their unique challenges and goals.

At Sun Strategy Group, our mission is to empower businesses with actionable insights and strategic direction while providing University of Toronto students with invaluable hands-on consulting experience.
