About Us

01 — Where We StartedOur Story

Sun Strategy Group was founded by a group of University of Toronto students who saw a lack of accessible and affordable consulting services provided to local businesses around Toronto and the GTA. We believe that all businesses deserve access to high quality strategic guidance, regardless of their size or budget. Through dedicated research, time, and persistence, we promise to deliver high-quality results and solutions to every one of our clients.

02 — How We WorkOur Approach

We operate on a project by project model, where each undertaking is personalized and led by a dedicated team of students who will personally tailor each solution to your needs. Our approach comes from a deep understanding of business fundamentals, combined with a commitment to delivering timely and actionable results. From market research to operational planning, we work very closely with our clients via meetings and check ins to develop tailored solutions that address their unique challenges and goals.

At Sun Strategy Group, our mission is to provide local businesses with insightful solutions and strategic guidance to help them thrive, while also offering friendly, structured support throughout the entire process.
