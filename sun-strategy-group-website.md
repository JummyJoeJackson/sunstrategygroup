## About Us

### Our Story

> `Sun Strategy Group was founded by a group of University of Toronto students in 2025 who saw a lack of opportunities for undergraduate students to gain real-world consulting experience. We believe that all businesses deserve access to high-quality strategic guidance, regardless of their size or budget.`

### Our Approach

> `We operate on a project-based model, where each engagement is led by a dedicated team of students who bring fresh perspectives and innovative solutions to the table. Our approach is rooted in a deep understanding of business fundamentals, combined with a commitment to delivering measurable results. From market research to strategic planning, we work closely with our clients to develop tailored solutions that address their unique challenges and goals.`

### Mission Statement

> `At Sun Strategy Group, our mission is to empower businesses with actionable insights and strategic direction while providing University of Toronto students with invaluable hands-on consulting experience.`

### Values

- **Excellence:** `We are committed to delivering high-quality work that exceeds expectations`
- **Integrity:** `We operate with honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our engagements`
- **Collaboration:** `We believe in the power of teamwork and work closely with our clients to achieve shared goals`
- **Innovation:** `We embrace fresh perspectives and creative solutions to address complex business challenges`
- **Impact:** `We are committed to making a positive impact on our clients and our community`
